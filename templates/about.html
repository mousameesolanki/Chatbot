CHATBOT WORKING

You can click on chat icon in widget to start your screening tests through quizes.

Questions in these tests will be about physical health,wellbeing,autonomy,choice and control,self perceptions,hope and hopelessness ,relationship and belonging and activity.

A screening only takes a few minutes, and after you are finished you will be given information about the next steps you should take based on the results. A screening is not a diagnosis, but it can be a helpful tool for starting a conversation with your doctor or a loved one about your mental health.

Afte the question answer session of chatbot you will be asked for your email id and address.Via email we will provide you a ticket which contains guiding steps to follow next.
